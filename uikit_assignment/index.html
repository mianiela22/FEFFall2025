📖 Pet Care Guide 🌸

  • 🍕 Feeding Your Pet

    Feed Buddy when hunger is high! Each feeding decreases hunger by 30% and increases happiness by 15%. Buddy loves all kinds of yummy treats! 💕

    Playing increases happiness by 25% but uses 20% energy. Make sure Buddy isn't too tired before playing! Favorite games: fetch, tug-of-war, and hide & seek! 🎾

  • 😴 Sleep & Rest

    Sleep restores 40% energy but increases hunger by 10%. A well-rested pet is a happy pet! Let Buddy nap when energy is low. 💤

    Baths increase happiness by 20%! Keep Buddy clean and fresh. A clean pet is a confident pet! 🧼✨
